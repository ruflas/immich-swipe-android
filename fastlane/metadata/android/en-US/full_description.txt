Immich Swipe is an open-source Android application designed to make sorting your photos and videos hosted on your Immich server easy and fun.

Inspired by the concept of Tinder for photos, it allows you to quickly declutter your library using simple swipe gestures.

Key Features:
- Fast Sorting Stack: Swipe right to keep, left to delete.
- Snooze (SKIP): Pass media temporarily and set a reappearance delay.
- Deferred Sync: Nothing is deleted on your server without your final approval in the Review Mode.
- Session Save: Your progress in each album is automatically saved.
- Multi-Album Management: Perfectly handles media present in multiple albums.
- Connection Diagnostic: Real-time visual indicator of your server status.
- Modern Interface: Full Material You support with Light and Dark themes.
